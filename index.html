About Us

Welcome to the official Binghamton Bowling webpage. We at Binghamton Bowling are a student run club sports orginization with a passion for the sport of bowling. Take a look at what we have to offer! We have events and activities for bowlers of any skill level!

Sport Shot League

One of our most popular activities that we run is our weekly sport shot league. In the league teams of 4 compete against each other on different collegiate sport shot oil patterns to get the most points. The top 4 teams will then compete in a stepladder finals at the end of the season to see who the winner is. Teams are drafted at the beginning of the semester by captains who had the highest averages from last sport shot league. This is for bowlers that want some competition and want to test themselves on sport shot patterns. Sport shot patterns are more tricky oil patterns and bowling on them is a good way to imporve your game. We highly recommend all skill levels of bowlers to join the sport shot league. The friendly competition and excitement of the league makes this activity a blast! The league is $6 a week and it takes place on Mondays at 9pm. The sport shot league requires some comitment, signups are at the beginning of the semester and members are required to make most of the league nights. You don't want to let your team down.
