About Us

Welcome to the official Binghamton University Bowling webpage. We are a student run Club Sport organization with a passion for the sport of bowling. We offer events and activities to bowlers of all skill levels, ranging from social/recreational events all the way to the highest level of Collegiate bowling. Take a look at what we have to offer!

Wednesday Night Open Practice

Our Wednesday Night Open Practices isa a fun bowling environment where all students of Binghamton University are welcome. Wednesday nights are a great time to come down and enjoy bowling with friends and sharpen your skills. Throughout the semester we run social events and friendly competitions. This is a non-commitment event, so anyone can come at any time!

2022-2023 Price: $5 for 3 games and shoes included
Where: Ripic's Carousel Lanes
When: Wednesdays @ 9:30pm. Rides are given to those on campus from Mohawk Hall @ 9:10pm

Sport Shot League

One of our most popular activities that we run: our weekly BU Sport Shot League! In our league teams of 3 to 4 compete for 3 games a week on different collegiate sport shot oil patterns. Sport shot patterns are more tricky oil patterns, where your room for error is significantly decreased.

Bowlers of all skill levels are welcome to challenge themselves, improve their game, and have fun. Bowling on them is a great way to improve your game. Our league requires commitment. Members are required to make most of the league nights and pay for all the league weeks (even ones you don't attend).

2022-2023 Price: $6/week and shoes included
Where: Ripic's Carousel Lanes
When: Mondays @ 9:30pm. Rides are given to those on campus from Mohawk Hall @ 9:10pm

Competitive Team

The Binghamton Competitive Bowling Team looks to bring the highest level of collegiate bowling to our bowlers. We compete in numerous USBC Certified Collegiate tournaments throughout the school year to the best of our ability in order to make Sectionals. Skill evaluations are held each year in order to determine our roster. The college bowling season runs from September through February, with the postseason extending into April. For a complete schedule of our season, check out Our Calendar.
